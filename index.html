FAQ

OTP

01.
What is the OPT Series Powder Sampler best suited for?
The OPT Series is optimized for high-volume production environments requiring continuous sampling with minimal operator intervention and maximum reliability.
02.
What is the OPT Series Powder Sampler best suited for?
Yes, the OPT Series is designed for integration with automated systems and can be customized to match existing processing lines, including PLC integration for automated sampling control.
03.
What are the material options for the OPT Series?
The OPT Series is available in various materials including stainless steel grades (316L, 304), Hastelloy for corrosive applications, and specialized coatings for specific product requirements.
04.
What safety or ATEX certifications does the OPT Series have?
The OPT Series can be supplied with ATEX certification for explosive atmospheres, IP65 rating for dust protection, and meets various international safety standards including CE marking.

MP

01.
What is the MP Series Powder Sampler used for?
The MP Series Powder Sampler is designed for automated sampling of powdered materials in industrial applications, providing consistent and representative samples for quality control and process monitoring.
02.
What types of powders can the MP Series handle?
The MP Series can handle a wide range of powders including pharmaceutical ingredients, fine chemicals, and food-grade powders. It is suitable for free-flowing and semi-free-flowing powders.
03.
Is the MP Series Powder Sampler GMP compliant?
Yes, the MP Series is designed to meet GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) requirements with validated cleaning procedures and documentation support for regulatory compliance.
04.
How is the MP Series cleaned and maintained?
The MP Series features easy disassembly for thorough cleaning, with smooth surfaces and minimal crevices. Regular maintenance includes lubrication of moving parts and calibration checks.
